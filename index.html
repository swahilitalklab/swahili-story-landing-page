Juma anaenda shuleni kila siku asubuhi. Anachukua mkoba wake wa rangi ya samawati.

"Juma goes to school every morning. He carries his blue bag."

anaenda = goes kila siku = every day mkoba = bag samawati = blue
  • 1Listen and repeat each sentence aloud
  • 2Record yourself saying “Ninaenda shuleni”
  • 3Answer in Swahili: What colour is Juma's bag?
  • 4Make your own sentence using “kila siku”
  • 5Retell the page in your own words
